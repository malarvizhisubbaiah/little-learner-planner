# Free Worksheet Sources for Ages 3–4

## Top Recommended Sites

### 1. Education.com — Preschool Worksheets
- **URL**: https://www.education.com/worksheets/preschool/
- **What**: Thousands of free preschool worksheets (letters, numbers, shapes, colors)
- **Free tier**: 3 free downloads per month (account required)
- **Best for**: Letter tracing, number recognition, coloring pages

### 2. K5 Learning — Free Preschool Worksheets
- **URL**: https://www.k5learning.com/free-preschool-kindergarten-worksheets
- **What**: Free printable worksheets for preschool and kindergarten
- **Free tier**: All worksheets are free, no account needed
- **Best for**: Counting, letter recognition, shapes, patterns

### 3. Worksheet Fun
- **URL**: https://www.worksheetfun.com/category/preschool/
- **What**: Colorful, engaging worksheets for preschoolers
- **Free tier**: All free, no account needed
- **Best for**: Tracing, patterns, shapes, beginning sounds

### 4. Super Teacher Worksheets — Pre-K
- **URL**: https://www.superteacherworksheets.com/pre-k.html
- **What**: High-quality printable worksheets
- **Free tier**: Some free, premium subscription for full access
- **Best for**: Alphabet, counting, fine motor skills

### 5. Teachers Pay Teachers — Free Pre-K
- **URL**: https://www.teacherspayteachers.com/Browse/Price-Range/Free/PreK
- **What**: Teacher-created resources, many free
- **Free tier**: Thousands of free resources (account required)
- **Best for**: Themed activity packs, seasonal worksheets

### 6. Twinkl — EYFS Resources
- **URL**: https://www.twinkl.com/resources/early-years
- **What**: UK-based educational resources, very high quality
- **Free tier**: Some free resources (account required)
- **Best for**: Phonics, number formation, tracing

### 7. Kindergarten Worksheets and Games
- **URL**: https://www.kindergartenworksheetsandgames.com/preschool-worksheets/
- **What**: Free printable preschool worksheets
- **Free tier**: All free
- **Best for**: Alphabet, counting, colors, shapes
